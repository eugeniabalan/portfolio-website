About me

My Story

Who am I? I am an UI designer and explorer. Passionate about illustration, human behaviour and in love with being creative. I lived in more than five different cultures until now and travelled almost all around the world. My background in education, psychology, healthcare, tourism and copywriting shaped the person I am today: communicative, an active listener, emphatetic, curious about the world and people who live in it.I love creating clean, elegant, original and functional designs. I design for people and with people in mind. I saw the world with other eyes since I entered the design world. I started paying attention to the smallest details around me and looking for inspiration everywhere. Design is a part of my daily life now. And it's a great pleasure to explore it in so many different ways! I am currently available for a remote contract in Germany.
