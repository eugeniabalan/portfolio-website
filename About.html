Who Am I?

I am an UI designer and explorer. Passionate about User Centered Design, Design Thinking, human behaviour and in love with being creative. I lived in more than five different cultures until now and travelled almost all around the world.

My background in education, psychology, healthcare, tourism and copywriting shaped the person I am today: communicative, an active listener, empathetic, curious about the world and people who live in it.

I love creating clean, elegant, original and functional designs. I design for people and with people in mind. I saw the world with other eyes since I chose design as a career. I started paying attention to the smallest details around me and looking for inspiration everywhere. Design is a part of my daily life now and it's a great pleasure to explore it in so many different ways!

In my spare time you can find me often in my studio at home, illustrating real objects, sketching portrets or trying new paint materials. The deeper I dig into illustration world, the more affinities with design I find. It begins with exploring ideas, continues with narrowing down the concept, sketching the scene and everything that follows is just adding more and more details. I love this similarity! In addition to illustrating, I love travelling, discovering new cities, people and cultures. I use to say that travelling is one of the most important factor when it comes to widen our horizons.

I am currently available to new opportunities in User Interface Design. If you are interested in my experience, please don't hesitate to contact me or take a look at my resume.
