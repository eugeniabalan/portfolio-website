Who Am I?

I am an UI/UX designer and explorer. I lived in more than five different cultures until now and travelled almost all around the world.

My background in education, psychology, healthcare, tourism and copywriting shaped the person I am today: communicative, an active listener, empathetic, curious about the world and people who live in it.

I design for people and with people in mind. I saw the world with other eyes since I chose design as a career. I started paying attention to the smallest details and looking for inspiration everywhere.

In my spare time you can find me often in my studio at home, illustrating real objects, sketching portrets or trying new paint materials. The deeper I dig into illustration, the more affinities with design I find. It begins with exploring ideas, continues with narrowing down the concept, creating a composition and adding more and more details. I love this similarity! I also love travelling, discovering and exploring new cities, knowing new people and cultures.
